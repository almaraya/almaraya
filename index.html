AL MARAYA ALUMINIUM L.L.C

AL MARAYA ALUMINIUM L.L.C is a VAT-registered glass and aluminum company based in Ajman, UAE. As a specialized provider in the industry, we offer quality solutions for glass and aluminum products, dedicated to meeting the diverse needs of our clients in both commercial and residential projects.

  • We specialize in creating customized aluminum and glass solutions for various projects, including residential buildings, commercial complexes, retail spaces, and industrial sites.
  • Our team of experts brings years of experience and industry knowledge, ensuring each project meets both aesthetic and functional standards.
  • We pride ourselves on working closely with clients to deliver tailored solutions that align with their vision, project goals, and technical specifications.

For more information or to discuss your project needs, feel free to reach out to us. At AL MARAYA ALUMINIUM L.L.C, we are committed to helping you bring your vision to life with precision, quality, and unmatched expertise in glass and aluminum solutions.

Glass

Aluminium

Partition

High-performance facades that combine strength with sophistication, tailored to meet your project’s specifications.

Doors

Ideal for spaces that require seamless access and space-saving options, sliding glass doors offer a sleek look and are perfect for homes, offices, and retail spaces. They provide smooth operation with minimal space requirements, enhancing connectivity and visibility between spaces.

Frameless Glass Doors

Frameless designs create a minimalistic and contemporary look, offering maximum visibility and an unobstructed view. These doors are especially popular in modern architectural settings, like offices, showrooms, and luxury homes, adding sophistication without compromising on structural integrity.

Automatic Glass Doors

Often used in commercial spaces such as shopping centers, airports, and hotels, automatic glass doors offer convenience, accessibility, and a touch of luxury. Our automated solutions incorporate advanced sensors and mechanisms for smooth, hands-free operation.
